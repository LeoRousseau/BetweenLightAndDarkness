A swarm of moths to flutter around a lamp or candle flame.

Load "Lamp_Moths.unity" for an example scene, or simply drag the Lamp_Moths Prefabs folder (in the Project window) into the Hierarchy window, placing the node in the centre of your chosen lamp or flame.

Have fun!
